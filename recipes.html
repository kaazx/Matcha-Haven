RECIPES & DIY

matcha latte (classic & iced)

Ingredients:

  • 1 tsp matcha powder
  • 2 oz hot water (not boiling)
  • 6 oz milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • Sweetener (optional)

Steps:

  1. Sift matcha powder into a cup.
  2. Add hot water and whisk until smooth and frothy.
  3. Warm the milk and pour over matcha for a hot latte.
  4. For iced: add ice cubes, pour milk, then whisked matcha on top.

matcha salt for fries

Ingredients:

  • 2 tsp sea salt
  • 1 tsp matcha powder

Steps:

  1. Mix salt and matcha in a small bowl.
  2. Sprinkle over hot fries or popcorn.
  3. Store extra in an airtight container and enjoy the unique umami flavor!
