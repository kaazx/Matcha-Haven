HEALTH & WELLNESS

benefits of matcha

  • Supports metabolism and weight management
  • Promotes relaxation due to L-theanine
  • Enhances skin health with antioxidants
  • Strengthens the immune system

wellness tips

  • Morning: Swap coffee with matcha for a calmer start
  • Midday: Use matcha to avoid energy crashes
  • Evening: Try matcha smoothies for a lighter dinner

matcha comparisons

Beverage Caffeine (mg) Notable Benefits
Matcha 70 Calm focus, high antioxidants
Coffee 95 Strong energy boost
Green Tea 35 Gentle energy, mild antioxidants
Herbal Tea 0 Relaxing, caffeine-free
Energy Drink 120 Quick boost, often with sugar
  • Matcha: 70mg caffeine – Calm focus, high antioxidants
  • Coffee: 95mg caffeine – Strong energy boost
  • Green Tea: 35mg caffeine – Gentle energy, mild antioxidants
  • Herbal Tea 0mg caffeine – Relaxing, caffeine-free
  • Energy Drink: 120mg caffeine – Quick boost, often with sugar

Did you know? Matcha contains up to 137 times more antioxidants than regular green tea, particularly catechins that help fight cell damage.
